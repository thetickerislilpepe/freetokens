Scammer Tactics Explained

  • Phishing Links: Scammers send fake links on X, Discord, or email, mimicking trusted projects like Lumiterra.
  • Wallet Connection: Victims connect wallets to a malicious website.
  • Malicious Transaction: Approving a transaction drains funds to the scammer.

How to Stay Safe

  • Verify website domains (e.g., lumiterra.io, not lumiterra.xyz).
  • Use hardware wallets (Ledger, Trezor).
  • Check transactions on Etherscan, Solscan, or Ronin Explorer.
  • Use Wallet Guard or Trust Wallet scanners.
  • Avoid untrusted links.
